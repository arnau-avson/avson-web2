Why now

Bidding without ENS
is no longer an option.

Royal Decree 311/2022 establishes the National Security Scheme as a mandatory requirement for any company providing digital services to Spanish Public Administration. Without it, you are excluded from public tenders.

Direct exclusion from tenders
Public bodies include ENS as an eliminating requirement in their ICT contracts since 2022. Without the certificate, your bid is not even opened.
Adaptation window closed
The transition period is over. Companies without ENS are already losing contracts to certified competitors.
The category matters
Basic, Medium or High depending on the impact of your services. A free diagnosis tells you exactly what level you need and how long it takes.
Legal requirement

RD 311/2022 — BOE-A-2022-7191

"Public sector entities and their systems and services providers shall apply the ENS in the design and operation of their information systems."
