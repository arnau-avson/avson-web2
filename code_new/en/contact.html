Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions
about the diagnosis.

Is the initial diagnosis really free?
Yes, completely free and with no commitment. The first meeting lasts between 45 and 60 minutes. We analyse your current situation, identify which certifications you need and explain the complete process. You will not be asked for any payment details and you will not sign anything. At the end you receive a written diagnosis report, regardless of whether you work with us or not.
How long after I contact you do you get in touch?
Our commitment is to respond within 4 working hours. If you submit the form in the morning, you will have a reply before midday. If you do it in the afternoon, you will hear from us first thing the next day. For urgent cases (imminent tender deadlines, scheduled audits), email us directly at hello@avson.eu marking it as urgent.
What information do you need for the first meeting?
The more information you provide in the form, the more productive the first meeting will be. The minimum: industry sector, organisation size and the certification you are interested in (or the reason that brought you here, such as a public contract or a client requirement). You do not need any prior technical documents; our consultants will carry out the diagnosis during the meeting itself.
Is the meeting in person or by video call?
We work remotely by default via video conference (Teams, Google Meet or Zoom, according to your preference). This allows us to serve clients throughout Spain and at any time without travel costs. For large-scale projects or when the client prefers it, we can arrange in-person meetings in Madrid or travel to the client's premises.
Do you work with organisations of any size?
Yes. Our portfolio ranges from SMEs with 10 employees to business groups with several subsidiaries. Packs and methodology are adapted to the size. A small company can certify in 3 months with a limited scope; a large company may need a multi-year programme with phased implementation. The free diagnosis serves precisely to correctly size the project from the start.
